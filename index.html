My Tools

I mainly use Lunacy to plan out and experiment with prototypes, and most of my design prototypes here are made or planned out with it. For custom icons and raster graphics, I use Adobe Photoshop.

What I Prioritize in My Designs

BREATHABILITY

My most important element in designs is whitespace. When the context allows, I emphasize the space between elements, as I love my designs straightforward and easy on the eyes.
HIERARCHY

I make full use of weight, size, and order of elements. When my design comes across users, I want them to feel guided and less confused as possible.
MINIMALISM

I keep a consistently clean look in all of my designs. Solid fills, limited typefaces, and color schemes. Still in line with "easy-on-eyes", I believe it's simplicity and consistency that makes design stand out.
